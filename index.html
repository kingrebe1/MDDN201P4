Background to Project 4

This project began as an extension of Project 3, where I had made an online translator to translate Shakespearian passages into emoji stories.
Inspired by the Internet Yami-Ichi market featured on Back Streets of The Internet, I wanted to take elements of my original concept, but take it offline and back into print so it would be fitting to sell at a marketplace.
I decided to make a zine as by design they are copy pasted, and made to be easily redistributed by photocopier, something which happens online with emojipasta.
