A Midsummer Night's Zine:

The Incomplete Emoji Works of William Shakespeare

Background to Project 4

This project began as an extension of Project 3, where I had made an online translator to translate Shakespearian passages into emoji stories.
Inspired by the Internet Yami-Ichi market featured on Back Streets of The Internet, I wanted to take elements of my original concept and make it fit to sell at a marketplace, by taking it offline and back into print.
I decided to make a zine as by design they are cut and pasted, and made to be easily redistributed by photocopier, something which happens online with emojipasta emoji stories.
